## What We Do

MineTech is a holding company built on a single conviction: the way the world finds and funds mineral resources is broken. Traditional exploration is slow, expensive, and opaque. We are fixing that by bringing together artificial intelligence, blockchain infrastructure, and deep mining expertise under one roof.

Our focus is rare earth minerals — the critical inputs that power electric vehicles, defence systems, renewable energy hardware, and advanced electronics. Demand is accelerating. Supply chains are fragile. MTAL exists to close that gap.

## AI-Targeted Exploration

At the heart of MTAL's platform is a proprietary AI system that ingests satellite imagery, geophysical survey data, geochemical records, and historical drill results to build high-resolution subsurface probability models. Instead of relying on instinct or decades-old maps, our models identify high-probability deposit zones before a single drill is deployed.

This approach compresses exploration timelines from years to weeks. It also dramatically reduces capital waste — resources are committed only where the evidence is strongest. MTAL applies this intelligence across specific target regions, selected for their geological potential, jurisdictional stability, and strategic mineral profile.
