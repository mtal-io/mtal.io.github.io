About MTAL

The Intelligence Layer
Mining Has Been Waiting For

For too long, mineral exploration has relied on intuition, outdated surveys, and costly trial and error. MTAL changes that equation entirely — combining satellite imagery, geophysical data, and machine learning to predict deposit locations with unprecedented accuracy.

Once a project is viable, MTAL's blockchain layer opens it to global investors through tokenized participation — eliminating traditional barriers to mining investment while creating transparent, auditable returns.

Services

Three Products.
One Integrated Platform.

01

GeoScan AI

Multi-spectral satellite analysis combined with historical geological survey data. Our model identifies high-probability mineral deposit zones before a single drill is deployed.

  • Satellite + LiDAR fusion
  • Geochemical anomaly detection
  • Probability deposit mapping
  • Report generation in 72h
02

OptiMine

Real-time operational intelligence that continuously optimises blast patterns, haul routes, and processing throughput — reducing cost-per-tonne while maximising ore recovery.

  • Live fleet & equipment monitoring
  • Predictive maintenance alerts
  • Dynamic blast optimisation
  • Processing plant efficiency AI
03

MTAL Token

Each mining project is tokenised on-chain. MTAL token holders participate in project selection, receive proportional returns, and gain full transparency into project financials.

  • Fractionalized project ownership
  • On-chain revenue distribution
  • DAO governance voting rights
  • Secondary market liquidity
Solutions

From Discovery
to Distribution

I

Scan & Analyse

GeoScan AI processes satellite, geochemical, and historical data to build a 3D subsurface probability model of the target region.

II

Validate & Model

Our geologists and engineers review AI outputs, conduct targeted verification drilling, and build the project's extraction model and financial projections.

III

Tokenise & Fund

Validated projects are tokenised on the MTAL blockchain layer. Global investors acquire tokens representing proportional project stakes.

IV

Mine & Distribute

OptiMine optimises ongoing operations. Revenue is automatically distributed to token holders via smart contract on a quarterly basis.
